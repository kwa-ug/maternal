Don'ts During Pregnancy

Things to avoid during pregnancy

Below are some of the things that are harmful to you and your baby.

Try to lose weight while still pregnant.

Cutting out food groups to lose weight may stop your baby from getting nutrients they need for growth. Instead of restricting the diet, you can manage your weight through eating a healthy and balanced diet with a variety of foods from each of the main groups.

Eating all kinds of food.

Avoid certain foods during pregnancy. Some foods carry a small risk of infections during pregnancy. These infections are rare but can cause problems for the developing baby. Examples include; raw or uncooked meat, liver and other foods containing vitamin A, raw eggs or undercooked eggs etc. Avoid caffeine.

Smoking and taking alcohol.

Smoking increases the risks of complications like miscarriage, stillbirth and premature birth. There is no known safe level for drinking alcohol, so itâ€™s best to void it completely during pregnancy. Quit smoking and taking alcohol.

Continuous taking of Coffee.

High levels are linked to pregnancy complications. It is best to limit your coffee or caffeine intake as much as possible.
