For a healthy baby

The following key components are recommended for a healthy lifestyle during pregnancy

  • Appropriate weight gain
  • A balanced diet
  • Regular exercise
  • Appropriate and timely vitamin and mineral supplementation.

Ideal foods to eat during pregnancy

Vegetables

These include carrots, cooked greens, pumpkin, spinach, sweet potatoes, red sweet peppers. The benefits are Vitamin A and Potassium.

Fruits

These include apricots, bananas, cantaloupe, grapefruit,honeydew, mangoes,oranges, prunes, tomatoes.The benefits are Potassium.

Diary

These include skim or 1% milk, soymilk, fat-free or low-fat yogurt. The benefits are Vitamin A, Vitamin D, Calcium and Potassium.

Grains

These include ready to eat cereal or cooked cereal. The benefits are Iron and Folic Acid.

Proteins

These include beans, peas, lean beef, lamb, pork, nuts, seeds, poultry, salmon, trout, herring, sardines and pollock. The benefits are Amino Acid.

Foods to avoid

The following food should be avoided during pregnancy

  • Unpasteurized milk and foods
  • Hot dogs and luncheon meat
  • Raw and undercooked seafood, eggs and meat.Do not eat sushi made with raw fish
  • Refrigerated smoked seafood
  • Avoid all forms of alcohol

Prenatal vitamin and mineral supplements

Folic acid

All women of childbearing age should consume 400 micrograms of folic acid. Folic acid nutrient is found in;

  • Some green leafy vegetables
  • Most berries, nuts, beans, citrus fruits
  • Some vitamin supplements

Dietary and caloric recommendations

To maintain a healthy pregnancy, approximately 300 extra calories are needed each day. The calories should come from a balanced diet of proteins, fruits, vegetables and whole grains. Sweets and fats should be kept to a minimum. A well balanced diet can reduce symptoms such as nausea and constipation.

Fluid intake during pregnancy

Take enough fluids by drinking several glasses of water plus juices and soups. Talk to the midwife or doctor about restricting your intake of caffeine and artificial sweeteners.
